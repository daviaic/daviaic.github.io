Currently, I am a PhD student at Gran Sasso Science Institute, in the Computer Science area.

Before starting my PhD at GSSI, I received my B.Sc. in Mathematics and M.Sc. in Computer Science from Federal University of Ceará, Brazil, in 2022 and 2024, respectively.

My research interests are (mainly) algorithms, temporal graphs, combinatorial optimization and fair division.

My personal interests include Clube Atlético Mineiro (even if it's a love-hate relationship), music, brazilian politics and traveling.
